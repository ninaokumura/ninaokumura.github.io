Nina's learning journey

Learning plan

I decided to join this Bootcamp to switch career. I was working as an ECE teacher and It was definitely a great experience, where I could learn a lot from my coworkers and also from the kids. But at certain point I realized it was time to go out of my comfort zone and try something new.

I started studying programming in January but at a very slow pace. So when I heard about this course I thought it could be a great opportunity to learn faster and also to give me the motivation I needed to keep going.

One of my strengths in relation to learning is focus and organization. During foundations I've been using a weekly plan to guide me through all the tasks that needs to be done.

I expect that until the end of this course I can improve the skills I already have and also gain new ones, such as, effective communication and critical thinking.

I know that this will be a short but a intense learning journey. So, in order to keep my mind healthy I will try my best to remember to always be kind with myself and also to respect my limitations.

And hopefully I will get my first job and eventually be able to work remotely and have more time to go visit my family in Brazil.

Reflections

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

It would be amazing if I can work with something I enjoy but my main long term goal is to be able to have more flexibility while working. This way I could go back to Brazil and spend more time with my family.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I think my main strength is the ability to keep focus on what I am learning. On the other hand, I imagine that the learning pace is very fast during Bootcamp, so I might feel overwhelmed by that.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I consider myself a shy person, so it will be challenging to speak in front of others.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I hope the Bootcamp team can helps us create a enjoyable learning environment and guide us through all the challenges we might have.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

I hope by the end of this Bootcamp I can feel more confident with my communication and technical skills.
