Emotional Intelligence vs

Emotional intelligence is the ability to recognize and understand not only your own emotions but also helps to perceive how the others around you are feeling as well. It is a good practice to better manage these emotions in order to overcome future challenges.

Dan Goleman is a psychologist who is responsible for turning the concept of emotional intelligence popular with his book Emotional Intelligence in 1996.

According to Goleman there are 5 component's to help you improve your emotional intelligence:

Self awareness

Self awareness is all about learning to better understand your emotions and thoughts. Once we can fully understand what is going on in our lives we can then start making some improvements to control these emotions instead of let them control us.

Self regulation

Self regulation is about staying in control of the situation. You need first understand who you are and how do you behave when facing a challenge. It is a good practice if you want your mind to be more calm.

Motivations

Self motivation is definitely not an easy skill to put in practice. We are all humans and of course we have our struggles, so specially if you are learning something new by yourself you gotta need some guidance to help you to stay on track.

For me, what really helps me stay motivated is to keep my goals clear. So this way I can kind of "trick" my brain to believe that even though the path is hard I will eventually achieve my goals if I don't give up.

Empathy

Empathy is the ability to understand the other people's emotions and thoughts. This skill helps us build strong relationships once we can truly understand the angsts of those around us.

Social skills

In order to have a good social skills you need to reflect on how do you usually resolve the conflicts in your life, how can you motivate others around you and also how can you improve your communication skills in social situations.

So emotional intelligence can helps us be more aware of everything that surround us and can also help us to become better humans.
