Hello world

First of all, I am glad that we all made to the second week. Well done! That is certainly a victory already.

I decided to join this Bootcamp to switch career. I was working as an ECE teacher and It was definitely a great experience, where I could learn a lot from my coworkers and also from the kids. But at certain point I realized it was time to go out of my comfort zone and try something new.

I started studying programming in January but at a very slow pace. So when I heard about this Bootcamp I thought it could be a great opportunity to learn faster and also to give me the motivation I needed to keep going.

One of my strengths in relation to learning is focus and organization. During foundations I've been using a weekly plan to guide me through all the tasks it needs to be done.

I expect that until the end of this course I can improve the skills I already have and also gain new ones, such as, effective communication and critical thinking.

I know that this will be a short but a intense learning journey and I tend to feel easily overwhelmed. So, in order to keep my mind healthy I will try my best to remember to always be kind with myself and also to respect my limitations.

And hopefully I will get my first job and eventually be able to work remotely and have more time to go visit my family in Brazil. I miss them a lot!
